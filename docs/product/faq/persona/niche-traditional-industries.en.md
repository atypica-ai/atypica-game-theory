## Question Type
Product Q&A (TYPE-A)

## User's Real Concerns
- I'm in food service/manufacturing/agriculture, does the AI Persona library have users from these industries?
- The 300K personas are mainly internet/tech users, right?
- Can AI understand users in niche industries?

## Underlying Skepticism
Concern about persona library coverage scope

## Core Answer

**Answer: Yes. And atypica's power lies not in ready-made personas, but in having a construction methodology.**

Even if the library doesn't have a perfect match, we can build personas through:
1. **Social Media Scanning**: Build personas from niche social platforms/forums
2. **Custom Personas**: Upload your real user data

## Three Solutions

### Solution 1: Targeted Construction via Social Media Scanning

**Applicable Scenario**: Niche industry, insufficient personas in library

**Process**:
1. Find social platforms/forums where target users are active
   - Food service: Dianping, Xiaohongshu restaurant reviews
   - Manufacturing: LinkedIn, industry forums
   - Agriculture: Agricultural forums, WeChat groups

2. Social Media Scanning conducts 10-15 rounds of deep analysis
   - Extract real user behavioral patterns
   - Analyze decision logic and pain points

3. Auto-generate Public Personas or Custom Personas
   - Complete in 3-5 days
   - Consistency reaches 80-85 points (close to or exceeds real human baseline 81)

**Real Case**:

**Client A: Agricultural Supply Dealer**
- Need: Understand farmers' acceptance of new fertilizers
- Library status: Only 20 farmer personas (insufficient)
- Solution:
  - Social Media Scanning "Toutiao Agriculture Channel," "Agricultural Forums"
  - After 10 rounds of observation, generated 30 farmer personas
  - Coverage: Large grain farmers, small farmers, organic agriculture practitioners
- Time: 5 days
- Result: Client satisfaction 4.5/5.0

---

### Solution 2: Custom Persona Import

**Applicable Scenario**: Already have real user data

**Data Sources**:
- CRM customer data
- Historical interview records
- User research questionnaires
- Customer service conversation logs

**Process**:
1. Export your user data (Excel/CSV/PDF)
2. Upload to atypica
3. System automatically converts to Custom Personas
4. Quality score: Depends on data completeness

**Quality Expectations**:
| Data Type | Quality Level | Notes |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| In-depth interview records (5000 words) | High | Highest quality |
| CRM purchase records + basic info | Medium | Medium quality |
| Only basic information form | Low | Not recommended |

**Real Case**:

**Client B: Food Service Chain Brand**
- Need: Test new dish concepts
- Library status: Has general "restaurant customers," but not their own customers
- Solution:
  - Import 500 members' CRM data (consumption records + basic info)
  - Generate 500 Custom Private Personas
  - Test new dishes based on real customers
- Time: 1 day import, immediately usable
- Result: Feedback more aligned with real customers

## Niche Industry FAQ

### Q1: If my industry is very niche (e.g., handicrafts), what do I do?

**Solution**:
1. Social Media Scanning Xiaohongshu/Douyin and other platforms' related content
2. Or import your existing customer data
3. If there's completely no data, recommend doing small-scale real human interviews first (5-10 people), then import to generate AI Personas

### Q2: If my target users don't use social media (e.g., elderly people), what do I do?

**Solution**:
1. **Solution 2 priority**: Import your customer data
2. **Solution 1 secondary**: Observe children/family members' discussions (generational perspective)
3. **Real human interviews**: For special groups, recommend doing real human interviews first, then generate personas

### Q3: Will personas become outdated?

**Yes. Recommendations**:
- Rapidly changing industries (tech): Use personas built within last 6 months
- Stable industries (traditional): Personas from last 2 years still usable
- Custom Private Personas: Can periodically update data
