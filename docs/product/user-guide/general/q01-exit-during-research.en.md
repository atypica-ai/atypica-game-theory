# If I exit during research, will it terminate?

## Question Type
User Manual Question

---

## Quick Answer

**No, research continues running in the background.**

**Key Features**:
- ✅ **Continues in Background**: AI continues completing research after you exit
- ✅ **Return Anytime**: View completed content when you come back
- ✅ **Notification Reminders**: Get notified when research is complete
- ✅ **Fully Saved**: All conversations and results are automatically saved

**Simply put**:
- Like sending an email—you can close the browser after sending, the email still delivers
- atypica research works the same: you can exit after starting, AI keeps working

**Yes, 100% certain.**

- AI tasks run on server, not in your browser

**Verification method**:
- Initiate a research → Immediately close browser

**Won't affect already started research.**

**Sent messages**:

**Unsent messages**:
- ⚠️ Stored in local browser
