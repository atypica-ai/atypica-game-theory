# How to View My Usage Records and Billing?

## Question Type
User Manual Question

---

## Quick Answer

**View usage records and billing in account settings.**

**Quick Operations**:
```
Step 1: Click avatar in top right
Step 2: Select "Account Settings"
Step 3: Click "Usage Statistics" or "Billing Management"

Can view:
✅ Usage statistics: Interview, Scout, report, podcast counts
✅ Quota balance: Remaining quota for current period
✅ Billing history: All payment records
✅ Invoice management: Download and issue invoices
```

### Invoice Management

**Supported Invoice Types**:

**Personal Invoice**:
```
Type: VAT ordinary invoice (electronic)
Header: Individual

Purpose: Personal reimbursement

Application Process:
Step 1: Billing Management → Invoice Management

- Header: Individual

```
Type: VAT special invoice (deductible)
Header: Company name
Content: Information technology service fee
Purpose: Corporate reimbursement, tax deduction

Application Process:
Step 1: Billing Management → Invoice Management
Step 2: Select bills to invoice
Step 3: Fill company information:
- Company name
- Tax ID
- Bank and account number
- Company address and phone

Step 6: Paper invoice mailed in 5-7 business days

## FAQ

### Q1: How Long Are Usage Records Retained?

**Retained permanently.**

**Retention Content**:
```
Retained Permanently:
✅ All usage records (Interview, Scout, Reports)
✅ All billing records
✅ All invoice records

Value:
- Review historical usage anytime
- Financial audit and reimbursement
- Annual usage analysis
```

---

### Q2: When Does Quota Reset Each Month?

**Resets at 00:00 on the 1st of each month (Beijing time).**

**Reset Rules**:
```
Free/Professional Version:
- Resets at 00:00 on 1st of each month
- Unused quota from previous month does not accumulate

Team Version:
- Unlimited, no reset needed
- Podcast quota resets on 1st of each month (10/month)

Example:
Used 3 Interviews in January, 2 remaining
February 1st: Quota resets to 5 (not 7)
```

---

### Q3: Can Usage Records Be Exported?

**Yes, supports CSV and PDF formats.**

**Export Operations**:
```
Step 1: Go to Usage Statistics
Step 2: Select time range
- This month
- Last 3 months
- Last 6 months
- Custom period

Step 3: Select export format
- CSV: Suitable for Excel analysis
- PDF: Suitable for printing and archiving

Step 4: Download file

File Content:
- Date and time
- Feature type (Interview, Scout, etc.)
- Project name
- Usage duration
- Quota consumed
```

---

### Q4: Can Bills Be Modified?

**Cannot modify, but can apply for re-issuance.**

**If Need to Modify**:
```
Common Situations:
1. Invoice header incorrect
   → Apply for re-issuance (original invoice voided)

2. Amount error
   → Contact customer service to verify and re-issue

3. Invoice information missing
   → Apply for supplementary invoice

Operations:
→ Billing Management → Find bill
→ Click "Apply for Re-issuance"
→ Fill correct information
→ Submit application
→ Customer service reviews and processes (1-3 business days)
```

---

### Q5: Can Team Members View Team Bills?

**No, only administrators can.**

**Permission Division**:
```
Administrator:
✅ View team bills
✅ View team usage statistics
✅ Manage payment methods
✅ Apply for invoices

Regular Members:
✅ View their own usage records
❌ Cannot view team bills
❌ Cannot view team total usage
❌ Cannot view other members' detailed records

Viewer:
✅ View their own usage records
❌ Cannot view anything else
```

---

### Q6: What Happens If Forget to Renew?

**Account will downgrade, but data will not be lost.**

**Consequences of Forgetting to Renew**:
```
Professional → Free:
❌ Lose unlimited quota
✅ Revert to 5 Interviews/month, 2 Scouts/month
✅ All historical data retained
✅ Can re-subscribe anytime to restore

Team → Professional/Free:
❌ Lose team collaboration features
❌ Members lose access
✅ Administrator data retained
✅ Can re-subscribe anytime to restore
```

**How to Avoid**:
```
Recommendations:
✅ Enable auto-renewal (default)
✅ Ensure sufficient balance in payment method
✅ Bind multiple payment methods (backup)
✅ Enable email reminders (7 days before expiration)
```

---

### Q7: Can Request Refund?

**Has refund policy, but with conditions.**

**Refund Policy**:
```
7-Day No-Reason Refund:
✅ Within 7 days of subscription
✅ Used less than 3 Interviews
✅ Full refund

After 7 Days:
❌ Generally no refund support
⚠️ Special cases negotiable (such as system bug causing unusability)

Application Process:
Step 1: Billing Management → Apply for Refund
Step 2: Fill refund reason
Step 3: Customer service review (1-3 business days)
Step 4: Refund to original payment method (5-7 business days)
```

---

### Q8: Can Invoices Be Issued Retroactively?

**Yes, can issue within 1 year.**

**Retroactive Process**:
```
Step 1: Billing Management → Invoice Management
Step 2: Find uninvoiced bill
Step 3: Click "Apply for Invoice"
Step 4: Fill invoice information
Step 5: Submit application
Step 6: Issued in 1-3 business days

Limitations:
- Can only issue for bills within 1 year
- Cannot issue beyond 1 year
- Recommend applying for invoice promptly
```
