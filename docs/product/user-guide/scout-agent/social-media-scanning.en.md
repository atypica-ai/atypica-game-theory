# How to Have You Scan Social Media for Target Users?

## Question Type
User Manual Question

---

## Quick Answer

**3 Steps to Start Social Media Scanning**:
1. **Create Scout Task**: Describe what demographic you want to scan
2. **AI Starts Scanning**: Scout automatically scans social media for 5-10 rounds
3. **View Output**: Demographic profile + 3 high-quality custom AI personas (can be directly used for Interview)

**Time**: 1-2 days to automatically complete
**Output**: Structured demographic profile + high-quality AI personas

---

## Complete Operation Flow

### Step 1: Access Social Media Scanning Feature

**Entry Points**:
- Homepage → "Start New Research"
- Or Sidebar → "Social Media Scanning"
- Or directly visit `/scout/new`

---

### Step 2: Describe Scanning Target

**Input Box**: "Which users do you want to scan?"

**Example Inputs**:

**Example 1**: Product Testing Scenario
```
"Scan users discussing sparkling coffee on Xiaohongshu,
understand their attitudes toward this product category"
```

**Example 2**: New Market Entry
```
"Scan users in Chengdu discussing coffee consumption,
understand differences between Chengdu and Beijing users"
```

**Example 3**: Demographic Insights
```
"Scan 25-35 year old women discussing healthy snacks,
understand their consumer psychology and pain points"
```

**Input Tips**:

✅ **Good Description**:
- Clear demographic: Age, region, characteristics
- Clear topic: Discussing what content
- Clear purpose: Want to understand what

❌ **Avoid**:
- Too broad: "Scan young people" (What's young? What content?)
- Too narrow: "Scan users who bought our product" (Cannot find specific users on social media)
- Too vague: "See how the market is" (Need specific direction)

## Advanced Settings (Optional)

### Setting 1: Specify Scanning Platforms

**Default**: AI auto-selects platforms based on demographic characteristics

**Manual Specification**:
```
Platform Selection:
- Xiaohongshu (Young women consumer goods)
- Weibo (Tech products, hot topics)
- Bilibili (Young people, anime, tech)
- Douyin (Gen Z, short videos)
- X/Twitter (Overseas market, tech circles)
- Reddit (Overseas market, deep discussions)
```

**Recommendation**:
- Unsure which to choose → Let AI recommend
- Have clear target demographic → Manually specify 2-3 platforms

---

### Setting 2: Scan Rounds

**Default**: 10 rounds (Recommended)

**Adjustable**:
- 5 rounds: Quick understanding (suitable for urgent situations)
- 10-15 rounds: Deep understanding (recommended)
- 20+ rounds: Not recommended (diminishing returns)

**How to Choose**:
- Simple demographic (like young women consumer goods) → 10 rounds
- Complex demographic (like enterprise decision-makers) → 15 rounds
- Niche market (like figurine collectors) → 15 rounds

---

### Setting 3: Focus Dimensions

**Default**: Comprehensive scanning

**Can Focus**:
```
Dimension Selection:
- Price sensitivity (Focus on price-related discussions)
- Usage scenarios (Focus on when/where used)
- Competitor comparison (Focus on comparison attitudes with competitors)
- Pain point digging (Focus on complaints and dissatisfaction)
```

**Example**:
```
User: "Scan sparkling coffee users, focus on price sensitivity"

Scout will:
- Prioritize scanning price discussions
- Analyze acceptance across different price ranges
- Output detailed pricing recommendations
```

## FAQ

### Q1: How Long Does Social Media Scanning Take?

**Time**:
- Scanning process: 1-2 days (10 rounds scanning + AI reasoning)
- Can run in background automatically, no manual participation needed

**If Need Rush**:
- 5 rounds scanning: Half day to 1 day
- But depth and accuracy will decrease

---

### Q2: Can Pause or Adjust Midway?

**Yes**:
- Pause scanning: Save current progress
- Adjust direction: Modify scanning focus
- Increase rounds: If feel not deep enough

**Example**:
```
User after 5 rounds scanning:
"I want to add 5 rounds, focus on price sensitivity"

Scout will:
- Retain first 5 rounds' scanning results
- Scan 5 more rounds, focus on price discussions
- Integrate output
```

---

### Q3: Is Scanned Content Public?

**Completely Public**:
- Scout only scans publicly available social media content
- Does not access any private information
- No privacy concerns

**Data Sources**:
- Xiaohongshu public posts
- Weibo public discussions
- Bilibili public comments
- Other social platform public content

### Q4: What's the Quality of Generated AI Personas?

**Quality Level**: High-quality custom personas
- High consistency: Close to real person performance
- Depth: Built based on real user content
- Usability: Directly use for Interview/Discussion

**vs Temporary Generation**:
- Temporary generation: Lower consistency, shallow feedback

**Recommendation**:

**Example 2**: Regional filtering

Scout will:

- Don't filter too broad (too much noise)

## Best Practices

### Recommendation 1: Scout First Then Interview

**Recommended Flow**:
```
Step 1: Social media scan 10 rounds → Generate demographic profile
Step 2: Select AI personas based on profile
Step 3: Interview deep conversations for validation
```

**Value**:
- Scout ensures correct direction
- Interview deeply digs based on real demographics
- Avoid "interviewing into thin air"

---

### Recommendation 2: Don't Over-Rely on Single Platform

**Recommendation**:
- Scan 2-3 platforms
- Cross-validate findings
- Avoid platform bias

**Example**:
- Xiaohongshu + Weibo + Bilibili
- See consistent trends → High credibility
- See contradictions → Need further validation

---

### Recommendation 3: Regularly Re-Scan

**Market is Changing**:
- Re-Scout after 3-6 months
- Discover trend changes
- Adjust strategy promptly

**Value**:
- Avoid decisions based on outdated understanding
- Seize emerging opportunities

---

### Recommendation 4: Focus on "Why" Not Just "What"

**Common Mistake**:
- Only record "users like A"
- Don't dig deep "why like A"

**Correct Approach**:
- Ask "why" when scanning
- Analyze underlying motivations
- Output insights not just phenomena
