# How to Start an Interview?

## Question Type
User Manual Question

---

## Quick Start (3 Steps)

### Step 1: Create Interview Project

**Entry Point**: Homepage → "Create New Interview"

**Fill in Information**:
- Project Name: e.g., "Sparkling Coffee New Product Test"
- Research Objective: Brief description (within 100 characters)
- Project Type:
  - Interview Chat (one-on-one in-depth interviews)
  - Discussion Chat (group discussion)

---

### Step 2: Select AI Personas (5-10 people)

**Method 1: Search from Public Library**
- Enter keywords: e.g., "25-35 years old female white-collar coffee"
- Filter criteria: age, gender, occupation, interests
- Recommended quantity:
  - Interview: 5-10 people
  - Discussion: 3-8 people

**Method 2: Use Recommended Personas**
- System automatically recommends based on your research objective
- Covers different user segments

**Method 3: Choose from "My Personas"**
- Previously saved or created Tier 3 personas
- Quick reuse

---

### Step 3: Prepare Interview Questions (3-5 questions)

**Sample Question Templates**:
```
1. What's your first impression of [product/concept]?
2. In what scenarios would you use it?
3. Can you accept this [price] point?
4. Compared to competitors, what are the advantages/disadvantages?
5. Any suggestions for improvement?
```

**Tips**:
- No need to design all questions; AI will intelligently follow up
- Focus on core questions (3-5 only)

**Click "Start Interview"**

---

## During Interview Execution

### Interview Chat (One-on-One)

**AI Auto-Executes**:
- Parallel interviews with 5-10 personas
- 7-10 conversation rounds per persona
- Real-time progress display

**You Can**:
- Monitor interview progress in real-time
- Adjust questions mid-way (for specific personas)
- End certain interviews early

**Estimated Duration**:
- 5 people: approximately 50-70 minutes
- 10 people: approximately 80-100 minutes

---

### Discussion Chat (Group Discussion)

**AI Moderates Discussion**:
- Brings together 3-8 personas simultaneously
- Facilitates opinion exchange
- Controls speaking pace

**You Can**:
- Watch discussion in real-time
- Insert new questions mid-way
- Adjust discussion direction

**Estimated Duration**:
- 30-40 minutes

---

## After Interview Completion

### Auto-Generated Report

**Content Includes**:
- Interview Summary: Key findings from each persona
- Highlight Dialogue Snippets: As evidence
- Consensus and Divergence: Auto-identified
- User Profiles: 7-dimension detailed information
- Improvement Recommendations: Actionable suggestions

**View Location**:
- Interview project detail page → "View Report"
- Or Homepage → "My Projects" → Select project

## Detailed Step Instructions

### Creating Interview Project

**Project Name Suggestions**:
- ✅ Descriptive: e.g., "Sparkling Coffee New Product Test"
- ✅ Include timing: e.g., "2024Q1 Membership System Redesign"
- ❌ Avoid vague: e.g., "Project 1", "Test"

**Research Objective**:
- Briefly describe what you want to learn
- Example: "Understand acceptance and pricing sensitivity of sparkling coffee among 25-35 year old working women"

**Project Type Selection**:
| Type | Use Case |
|------|----------|
| **Interview Chat** | Explore "why", deep dive |
| **Discussion Chat** | Compare viewpoints, quickly identify consensus and divergence |

---

### Selecting AI Personas

#### Method 1: Keyword Search

**Search Tips**:
- Demographics: age, gender, occupation, city
- Behavioral traits: fitness, coffee, skincare, finance
- Scenario traits: commuting, office, home, social

**Example**:
```
Search: "25-35 years old female white-collar Shanghai coffee"
Results: Returns matching Tier 1/2 personas
```

**Filter Criteria**:
- Age: 18-25, 25-35, 35-45, 45-55, 55+
- Gender: Male, Female
- Occupation: Product Manager, Programmer, Designer, Student, Stay-at-home Mom
- Interests: Tech, Beauty, Fitness, Food, Travel

---

#### Method 2: System Recommendations

**Auto-recommend Based on Research Objective**:
- Enter research objective
- System analyzes keywords
- Recommends best-matching 5-10 personas

**Example**:
- Research Objective: "Test sparkling coffee acceptance"
- Recommended Personas:
  - 2 health-conscious types (focus on ingredients)
  - 2 price-sensitive types (focus on value)
  - 1 social trendsetter type (focus on appearance)

---

#### Method 3: My Personas

**Quick Reuse**:
- Select from "My Personas" - previously saved or created personas
- Suitable for repeated research or continuous tracking

**Scenarios**:
- Certain research frequently uses the same set of personas
- Build "My Commonly Used Persona Library"

---

### Preparing Interview Questions

#### Question Design Principles

**Open-Ended Questions**:
- ✅ "What's your first impression of this product?"
- ❌ "Do you like this product?" (closed-ended)

**Specific Scenarios**:
- ✅ "In what scenarios would you use it?"
- ❌ "Would you use it?"

**Avoid Leading**:
- ✅ "What do you think of this price?"
- ❌ "You think this price is very reasonable, right?"

---

#### Question Template Library

**New Product Testing**:
1. What's your first impression of [product name]?
2. When you hear this name/see this product, what comes to mind?
3. In what scenarios would you use/purchase it?
4. Can you accept this [price] point? Why or why not?
5. Compared to products you usually use, what are the advantages/disadvantages?

**Feature Testing**:
1. What pain points/needs do you have in daily life?
2. How do you currently solve this problem?
3. Would [feature description] help you?
4. Compared to existing solutions, which do you think is better?
5. Any suggestions for improvement?

**Pricing Testing**:
1. What's your usual budget for this category?
2. What do you think of this [price] point?
3. At what price would you "buy immediately"?
4. At what price would you "hesitate"?
5. If the price increases/decreases, would your decision change?

## Interview Execution Tips

### Interview Chat Tips

**Parallel Management**:
- AI automatically conducts parallel interviews with 5-10 people
- You can monitor each interview's progress in real-time
- One failed interview doesn't affect others

**Mid-Way Adjustments**:
- If a persona's responses are particularly valuable, you can extend the interview
- If a persona goes off-topic, you can end early

**Save Draft**:
- You can save as draft during the interview
- Resume next time without losing progress

---

### Discussion Chat Tips

**Moderator Guidance**:
- AI moderator actively guides discussion
- Controls speaking pace, ensures everyone has opportunity to express
- Identifies opinion conflicts, actively facilitates in-depth discussion

**Insert Questions Mid-Way**:
- If discussion gets stuck on a point, you can insert new questions
- Adjust discussion direction

**End Conditions**:
- Auto-ends after opinions fully collide
- Or manually end discussion

## Common Questions

### Q1: Must I prepare interview questions?

**Recommended but not mandatory**.
- Prepare 3-5 core questions; AI will automatically follow up and expand
- If unprepared, you can choose "Exploration Mode" where AI auto-designs questions based on persona characteristics

---

### Q2: How many personas should I select?

**Recommended Quantity**:
| Research Type | Recommended Number |
|--------------|-------------------|
| **Interview** | 5-10 people |
| **Discussion** | 3-8 people |

**Reasons**:
- Less than 5: Incomplete coverage, may miss key viewpoints
- More than 10: Information overload, difficult to analyze

---

### Q3: Can I replace personas mid-interview?

**Interview Chat**:
- Yes. Failed interview personas can be replaced
- Doesn't affect other completed interviews

**Discussion Chat**:
- No. Cannot replace personas after discussion starts
- Recommend confirming persona selection beforehand

---

### Q4: When is the interview report generated?

**Auto-Generated**:
- Interview: Auto-generated after all interviews complete
- Discussion: Auto-generated after discussion ends

**Estimated Duration**:
- 5-10 minutes

---

## Best Practices

### Recommendation 1: Start with Small-Scale Testing

**First-Time Use**:
- Select 5 personas for Interview
- Familiarize with process and output results
- Then expand to 10 people

---

### Recommendation 2: Combined Use

**Best Strategy**:
- Phase 1: Interview deep dive (5-8 people)
- Phase 2: Discussion comparison validation (3-5 people)

**Advantages**:
- First understand individual motivations
- Then observe group opinion collisions
- More comprehensive insights

---

### Recommendation 3: Continuous Tracking

**Build "My Commonly Used Persona Library"**:
- Save frequently used high-quality personas
- Directly reuse for subsequent research
- Observe same set of personas' reactions to different products

**Scenarios**:
- Certain research frequently uses same set of personas
- Can compare reaction changes across different time periods
