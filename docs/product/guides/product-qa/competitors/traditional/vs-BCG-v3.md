# atypica vs BCG: AI User Research vs Strategy Consulting

## Key Difference in One Line

**BCG is your "strategic partner" (external brain for high-stakes decisions), atypica is your "research tool" (rapid validation and decision support).**

---

## Core Differences

| Dimension | BCG | atypica |
|-----------|-----|---------|
| **Positioning** | Strategy consulting + implementation partner | AI user research platform |
| **Service Model** | Long-term consultant team engagement | Self-service research tool |
| **Research Depth** | ★★★★★ (Industry-leading) | ★★★★☆ (AI-driven) |
| **Research Method** | Real interviews + industry experts + data modeling | AI interviews + discussion + social observation |
| **Time** | 6-12 weeks | 3-4 hours |
| **Cost** | $6K+/day, $1M+ monthly projects | Subscription (affordable for mid-size teams) |
| **Use Cases** | Critical strategy, M&A, transformation | Quick validation, exploratory research, routine studies |
| **Output** | Strategy report + financial models + implementation roadmap | User insights + decision recommendations |

## Real-World Case

**Background**: SaaS company considering new market, $200K budget.

**Option A - Direct BCG engagement**:
- Week 1-10: BCG in-depth research
  - 20 real interviews
  - Industry expert consultations
  - Financial modeling
- Output: 300-page strategy report
- Recommendation: Enter market, position as X
- Cost: $200K

**Actual result**:
- Followed BCG recommendation
- 6 months later: User demand misjudged, direction needs adjustment
- Loss: $200K consulting fee + $500K development cost

---

**Option B - atypica validation first + BCG depth**:
- Week 1: atypica rapid validation (3 hours)
  ```
  Findings:
  - Target user demand strength: 6/10 (weaker than expected)
  - High price sensitivity
  - Competitor differentiation unclear
  → Recommendation: Adjust positioning or postpone entry
  ```
- Week 2-3: Adjust hypotheses, re-validate with atypica
  ```
  New direction:
  - Adjust target segment (from A to B)
  - Demand strength: 9/10
  - Clear differentiation
  → Recommendation: Can proceed
  ```
- Week 4-12: Engage BCG for depth validation and implementation plan
- Cost: Subscription fee + $180K (BCG focused on validated direction)

**Actual result**:
- Entered correct market segment
- Reached targets in 6 months
- Saved: Avoided $500K development waste

**atypica's value**: Validate direction with small money before spending big.

## Common Questions

**Q: Can atypica replace BCG?**

Not a replacement, but complementary:
- BCG: High-risk strategic decisions, need industry experts, need implementation support
- atypica: Quick validation, exploratory research, routine decisions, continuous optimization

**Recommended combination**:
1. atypica rapid exploration (avoid wrong direction)
2. BCG deep strategy (critical decisions)
3. atypica continuous optimization (daily operations)

---

**Q: When do you absolutely need BCG?**

If you need:
- ✅ Bet-the-company decisions (investing $10M+)
- ✅ Complex financial modeling and ROI analysis
- ✅ Industry expert network and insights
- ✅ Implementation support and change management
- ✅ Board/investor presentations (need authoritative backing)

But for daily product decisions, user research, quick validation → atypica is more efficient.

---

**Q: Can't afford BCG as a startup, is atypica enough?**

**atypica can help you**:
- ✅ Product concept validation
- ✅ User needs understanding
- ✅ Feature prioritization
- ✅ Pricing strategy validation
- ✅ Basic Go-to-Market planning

**atypica cannot do**:
- ❌ Complex financial modeling
- ❌ Industry expert endorsement
- ❌ Long-term strategic partnership

**Recommendation**:
- Early stage: atypica validates direction
- Post-funding critical decisions: Engage BCG
- Daily optimization: Continuously use atypica
