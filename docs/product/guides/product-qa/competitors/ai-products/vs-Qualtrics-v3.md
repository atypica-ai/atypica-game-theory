**atypica's capability**:
- AI deep interviews (can probe "why")
- 3000+ word deep conversations per person
- Discover unexpected insights

**Case comparison**:

**Using Qualtrics**:
```
Survey results:
Q: Why did you churn?
A1: Too expensive (40%)

```

**Using atypica**:
```
Deep interviews:
```

**The difference**: Qualtrics tells you "what," atypica tells you "why" and "how to fix."
