# How to create my first research project?

## Question Type
User Manual Question

---

## Quick Start (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Access Create Page

**Entry Points**:
- Homepage → "Start New Study"
- Or sidebar → "Create Study"

---

### Step 2: Describe Research Needs

**Input Field**: "What do you want to research?"

**Example Inputs**:
```
"I want to test acceptance of new sparkling coffee product"
```

**Tips**:
- Describe in natural language
- 1-2 sentences is enough
- No need to be professional

**Click "Start"**

---

### Step 3: Plan Mode Conversation Clarification

**AI will ask you some questions** to help clarify research intent.

---

### Step 4: Review Research Plan

**AI generates complete plan** for your review and confirmation.

---

### Step 5: Start Execution

**Click "Confirm Start"**, AI automatically executes research.

---

## Detailed Creation Process

### Phase 1: Describe Needs (1 minute)

#### Example Inputs

**Product Testing**:
```
"Test my new product: zero-sugar sparkling coffee,
target users are 25-35 year-old female white-collar workers"
```

**User Insights**:
```
"Understand why users churn,
we are a fitness app"
```

**Market Analysis**:
```
"Analyze Gen Z attitudes and willingness to pay for sustainable fashion"
```

---

#### Input Tips

**✅ Good Inputs**:
- Clear and specific: State research object and goals
- Include background: Brief intro to your product/industry
- Mention constraints: If there are budget or time limits

**❌ Avoid Inputs**:
- Too vague: "Help me do market research"
- Too complex: "Analyze the entire industry future trends and our strategic positioning and..."
- Too simple: "Coffee" (AI doesn't know what you want to research)

---

### Phase 2: Plan Mode Clarification (3-5 conversation rounds)

#### Typical AI Questions

**Question 1: "Who is your target audience?"**

**Example Answers**:
- ✅ "25-35 year-old female white-collar workers in tier-1 cities, frequent coffee drinkers"
- ✅ "Not sure, help me analyze"
- ❌ "Everyone" (too broad)

---

**Question 2: "What aspects do you want to understand?"**

**Example Answers**:
- ✅ "Acceptance, pricing, packaging design"
- ✅ "Comprehensive understanding, your recommendation"
- ❌ "Everything" (needs to be more specific)

---

**Question 3: "Any special requirements?"**

**Example Answers**:
- ✅ "Limited budget, hope to complete within 2 hours"
- ✅ "Need to report to boss, want to use SWOT framework"
- ✅ "No special requirements"

---

**Question 4: "Any reference materials?"**

**Example Answers**:
- ✅ "Have product intro PPT" (upload file)
- ✅ "Have competitor website links"
- ✅ "No"

---

#### Clarification Process Example

**Conversation Flow**:
```
User: "Test sparkling coffee new product"

AI: "Okay, who is your target audience?"

User: "25-35 year-old female white-collar workers"

AI: "What aspects do you want to understand? Acceptance, pricing, or others?"

User: "Acceptance and pricing"

AI: "Any reference materials? Like product intro or packaging design draft?"

User: "Have product intro PPT" (uploads file)

AI: "Got it. I will design a research plan,
including user discussions and in-depth interviews to test acceptance and pricing.
One moment..."
```

---

### Phase 3: Review Research Plan (2-5 minutes)

#### AI Generated Plan Example

```
Research Plan: Sparkling Coffee New Product Test

Research Objectives:
- Test product acceptance
- Validate pricing strategy (¥15-20 range)
- Collect user feedback

Target User Profile:
- 25-35 year-old female white-collar workers in tier-1 cities
- Regular coffee drinkers (3+ times per week)
- Health-conscious, willing to try new products

Research Steps:

1. Define target users (10 minutes)
   - Screen 8 AI personas matching criteria
   - 2 price-sensitive types
   - 3 health-anxious types
   - 3 social early-adopter types

2. Group Discussion (30-40 minutes)
   - 8-person Discussion
   - Discuss product concept and pricing
   - Quickly identify acceptance and controversy points

3. In-depth Interviews (60-80 minutes)
   - Select 5 people for 1-on-1 Interview
   - Deep dive into purchase motivation
   - Test 3 pricing scenarios (¥15/¥18/¥20)

4. Generate research report (10 minutes)
   - Acceptance assessment
   - Pricing recommendations
   - Product optimization directions

Estimated time: 2-3 hours
Estimated cost: ~¥80-120 in tokens

Output:
- Complete research report (PDF)
- User interview records
- Actionable product and pricing recommendations
```

---

#### Review Checklist

**Check 1: Are research objectives correct?**
- Do they match your actual needs?
- Any important aspects missing?

**Check 2: Are target users correct?**
- Is the population definition accurate?
- Are screening criteria reasonable?

**Check 3: Are research methods appropriate?**
- Is the Discussion + Interview combination suitable?
- Is the number sufficient (8 discussion + 5 interviews)?

**Check 4: Are time and cost acceptable?**
- Estimated time: 2-3 hours
- Estimated cost: ¥80-120
- Within budget?

---

#### Your Options

**Option 1: Satisfied, start directly**
- Click "Confirm Start"
- AI executes immediately

**Option 2: Need adjustments**
- Click "Modify Plan"
- Tell AI what to change
- AI updates plan

**Option 3: Not satisfied, regenerate**
- Click "Regenerate"
- AI generates new plan based on needs

---

### Phase 4: Execute Research (Automatic)

**AI Starts Execution**:
- Executes step by step according to plan
- Real-time progress display
- Can check progress midway

**You Can**:
- Continue using other features
- Close the page (research continues)
- Wait for completion notification

## Advanced Tips

### Tip 1: Upload Reference Materials

**Supported Materials**:
- Product intro PPT
- Competitor links
- User feedback screenshots
- Interview records (if available)

**Benefits**:
- AI better understands your product
- Generated plan more precise
- Saves clarification time

**Example**:
```
User: "Test new product concept"
AI: "Can you share product intro?"
User: Uploads product intro PPT
AI: Reads and generates more precise research plan
```

---

### Tip 2: Specify Budget and Time Constraints

**State constraints upfront**:
```
User: "I want comprehensive market analysis,
but limited budget, hope to complete within 3 hours"

AI: "Understood. I'll design a streamlined plan,
prioritizing the most important parts"
```

**AI Will Adjust**:
- Reduce interview count
- Simplify research steps
- Focus on core questions

---

### Tip 3: Specify Research Framework

**If you have preferences**:
```
User: "Use SWOT framework to analyze our competitive advantages"

AI: "Okay, I'll design research plan using SWOT framework"
```

**AI Will Follow Your Specified Framework**:
- Design research steps
- Organize report structure
- Ensure output aligns with framework

---

### Tip 4: Reference Previous Research

**If not first time**:
```
User: "Similar to last research,
but testing different product"

AI: "Understood. I'll reference your last research structure,
adjusting for new product"
```

**Benefits**:
- Maintain research consistency
- Results comparable
- Save design time

## Common Questions

### Q1: How long does creating research take?

**Time Allocation**:
- Input needs: 1 minute
- Plan Mode clarification: 3-5 minutes
- Review plan: 2-5 minutes
- **Total: 5-10 minutes**

---

### Q2: Must I upload reference materials?

**Not required**.
- With materials: AI understands more accurately
- Without materials: AI infers from description

**Recommendation**:
- If available, best to upload
- If not, can still proceed

---

### Q3: Will Plan Mode ask many questions?

**Usually 3-5 questions**:
- Target audience
- Research scope
- Special requirements
- Reference materials

**If needs are clear**:
- May only ask 1-2 questions

**If needs are vague**:
- May ask 5-8 questions

---

### Q4: Can I skip Plan Mode and execute directly?

**Not recommended**:
- Plan Mode helps clarify needs
- Avoids wrong direction
- Saves execution time

**But if you're experienced**:
- Can provide very detailed needs
- Plan Mode will quickly generate plan
- Execute immediately after review

---

### Q5: Can I modify plan after generation?

**Yes**. You can:
- Modify research steps
- Adjust target users
- Change research methods
- Add or remove content

**How to Modify**:
- Click "Modify Plan"
- Tell AI what to change
- AI updates automatically

## Bottom Line

> "Creating research is simple: Describe needs → Plan Mode clarifies → Review plan → Start execution.
> Key is to answer AI questions honestly, don't pretend to know."

**Remember**:
- ✅ Describe needs in natural language
- ✅ Answer Plan Mode questions honestly
- ✅ Review plan before executing
- ✅ Upload reference materials (if available)
- ✅ Specify budget and time constraints
