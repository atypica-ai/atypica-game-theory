# First time using atypica, where should I start?

## Question Type
User Manual Question

---

## Quick Answer

**Start with Plan Mode, create your first study in 3 steps:**
1. Describe what you want to research (one sentence is enough)
2. AI generates a research plan for your review
3. Confirm and AI automatically executes

**Recommended first study**: Test a simple product concept or user question.

---

## Complete Getting Started Guide (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Register and Login

**Entry Point**: atypica.AI official website

**Registration Options**:
- Email registration
- Google account login
- WeChat/Enterprise WeChat login

**Free Credits**:
- New users receive trial credits
- Can complete 2-3 full studies

---

### Step 2: Create Your First Study

**Location**: Homepage → "Start New Study"

**Input** (simple description is enough):
```
Example 1: "I want to know what young people think about our brand"

Example 2: "Test acceptance of new sparkling coffee product"

Example 3: "Understand why users churn"
```

**Tips**:
- No need for professional description
- 1-2 sentences explaining what you want to research
- AI will fill in the details

---

### Step 3: Plan Mode Clarifies Requirements

**AI will ask you a few questions**:

**Question 1: "Who is your target audience?"**
- Example answer: "25-35 year-olds in tier-1 cities, white-collar workers"
- Or: "Not sure, help me analyze"

**Question 2: "What do you want to understand?"**
- Example answer: "Acceptance, pricing, packaging design"
- Or: "Comprehensive market opportunity understanding"

**Question 3: "Any special requirements?"**
- Example answer: "Limited budget, hope to complete within 2 hours"
- Or: "No, follow your recommendation"

**Tips**:
- Answer honestly, say "not sure" if uncertain
- AI adjusts plan based on your answers
- Usually 3-5 conversation rounds to complete clarification

---

### Step 4: Review Research Plan

**AI Generated Plan Example**:
```
Research Plan: Sparkling Coffee New Product Test

Objectives:
- Test product acceptance
- Validate pricing strategy
- Collect packaging design feedback

Steps:
1. Define target users (8 people)
   - 25-35 year-old female white-collar workers
   - Regular coffee drinkers, health-conscious

2. Group Discussion (30 minutes)
   - 8 people discuss product concept
   - Quickly filter acceptance

3. In-depth Interviews (5 people, 60 minutes)
   - 1-on-1 deep dive into purchase motivation
   - Test 3 pricing scenarios

4. Generate research report

Estimated time: 2-3 hours
Expected output: Product acceptance report + Pricing recommendations
```

**Your Choices**:
- ✅ **Satisfied** → Click "Confirm Start"
- ⚠️ **Need Adjustments** → Tell AI what to modify
- ❌ **Not Satisfied** → Click "Regenerate"

---

### Step 5: AI Automatic Execution

**Execution Process**:
- AI automatically executes research plan
- Real-time progress display
- Can check progress midway

**You Can**:
- Continue using other platform features
- Close the page (study continues)
- System will notify upon completion

**Completion Time**:
- 2-4 hours (depending on study complexity)

---

### Step 6: View Report

**Notification Methods**:
- System notification
- Email notification

**Report Location**:

- Click project → "View Report"

**Report Content**:
- Research summary

## Recommended First Study

### Recommendation 1: Simple Product Test

**Example**:
> "Test my new product concept: a zero-sugar sparkling coffee"

**Why Recommended**:
- Clear scope, easy to understand
- Completes in 2-3 hours
- Clear output (acceptance + recommendations)
- Quick experience of complete process

---

### Recommendation 2: User Attitude Research

**Example**:
> "Understand Gen Z attitudes toward sustainable fashion"

**Why Recommended**:
- Involves in-depth interviews and discussions
- Experience multiple research methods
- Understand atypica's core capabilities

---

### Recommendation 3: Churn User Analysis

**Example**:
> "Why are our users churning?"

**Why Recommended**:
- Real business problem
- Requires deep dive into "why"
- Experience AI probing capabilities

---

## NOT Recommended First Studies

### ❌ Avoid: Overly Complex Research

**Example**:
> "Comprehensive analysis of our strategy for entering new market"

**Why Not Recommended**:
- Scope too large, easy to get lost first time
- Requires 4-8 hours
- Too complex for beginners

**Recommendation**:
- Start with small questions
- Do complex research after getting familiar

---

### ❌ Avoid: Too Vague Research

**Example**:
> "Help me do market research"

**Why Not Recommended**:
- Too vague, AI needs multiple clarification rounds
- Beginners may not know how to answer
- Easy to waste time

**Recommendation**:
- Specifically describe what you want to research
- Even if simple, be clear

## Common Beginner Questions

### Q1: What if I don't know how to describe my needs?

**No problem**. You can:
- Use simplest language to describe
- "I want to know XXX"
- AI will clarify through conversation

**Example**:
```
User: "I want to know what users think"
AI: "What do you want to know users' opinions about? Product, brand, or features?"
User: "About our new product"
AI: "Okay, what is your new product?"
...(gradual clarification)
```

---

### Q2: How long does first study take to complete?

**Time Estimates**:
- Simple study: 2-3 hours
- Medium study: 3-5 hours
- Complex study: 4-8 hours

**Recommendation**:
- First time choose 2-3 hour simple study
- Do complex research after familiarizing with process

---

## Practical Tips

### Tip 1: Start with Small Questions

**Don't try to solve big problems right away**:
- ❌ "Comprehensive market analysis"
- ✅ "Test a product concept"

**Reason**:
- Small questions more focused
- Get quick feedback
- Build confidence

---

### Tip 2: Answer Plan Mode Questions Honestly

**Don't pretend to know**:
- AI asks: "Who is your target audience?"
- ❌ Make up: "25-35 white-collar workers" (actually unsure)
- ✅ Honest: "Not sure, help me analyze"

**Benefits**:
- AI will help you analyze
- Avoid wrong direction
- Save time

---

### Tip 3: Use Plan Mode to Learn

**Plan Mode is the best teacher**:
- See how AI designs research plans
- Learn research methods and approaches
- Understand what makes good research

**As usage increases**:
- 1st time: Completely rely on AI
- 5th time: Start understanding logic
- 10th time: Can propose adjustments
- 20th time: Become half research expert

---

### Tip 4: Review Report Examples

**Learning Resources**:
- View example reports provided by platform
- Reference other users' public research (if available)
- Understand what makes good insights

**Capability Enhancement**:
- Understand how to ask questions
- Learn how to interpret results
- Know what makes actionable recommendations
