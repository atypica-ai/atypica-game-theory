### Downgrade and Cancellation

**Downgrade**:
```
From Professional → Free Version:
- Immediate downgrade
- Paid fees non-refundable
- Takes effect after current cycle ends
- Excess data retained (read-only)

From Team → Professional Version:
- Immediate downgrade
- Team data becomes personal (retain your portion)
- Other members lose access rights
```

**Cancel Subscription**:
```
Step 1: Enter account settings
Step 2: Click "Cancel Subscription"
Step 3: Confirm cancellation

Result:
- Becomes invalid after current cycle ends
- Downgraded to free version
- Data retained (complies with free version limits)
```
