# When to Use AI Interview vs AI Research?

## Question Type
User Manual Question

---

## Quick Comparison

| Dimension | AI Interview | AI Research |
|-----------|--------------|-------------|
| **Goal** | In-depth user interviews | Comprehensive analysis and research |
| **Participants** | 5-10 AI personas | Multiple data sources (personas + social media + web) |
| **Output** | Interview records + insights | Research report + strategic recommendations |
| **Duration** | 50-70 minutes | 2-4 hours |
| **Use For** | Explore "why" | Market analysis, trend research |

---

## AI Interview (In-Depth Interviews)

### When to Use?

✅ **Exploratory Research**
- Why do users choose/abandon a product?
- What are users' real pain points?
- Deep motivations behind decisions?

✅ **Product Testing**
- New product concept testing
- Feature priority ranking
- Pricing strategy testing

✅ **User Insights**
- Differences across user segments
- Specific needs of niche markets
- Behavioral pattern identification

### Core Capabilities

**Deep Follow-Up**:
- "5 Whys" technique, layer by layer
- Unlimited "why" questioning
- Uncover deep motivations

**Parallel Efficiency**:
- Interview 5-10 people simultaneously
- Individual records for each
- Complete in 3-4 hours

**Output Content**:
- 3000+ word interview summary per person
- Highlight dialogue snippets
- Key findings and user profiles

---

## AI Research (Comprehensive Research)

### When to Use?

✅ **Market Analysis**
- Industry trend analysis
- Competitive comparison research
- Market opportunity exploration

✅ **Strategic Planning**
- New market entry evaluation
- Brand repositioning
- Product line planning

✅ **Comprehensive Insights**
- Multi-dimensional data integration
- Macro trends + micro users
- Strategic-level decision support

### Core Capabilities

**Multi-Source Data Integration**:
- AI persona interviews
- Social media observation (Scout)
- Public web data
- Industry report analysis

**In-Depth Analysis**:
- Market size and growth trends
- Competitive landscape and opportunities
- User needs and pain points
- Product positioning and strategic recommendations

**Output Content**:
- Research report (20-30 pages)
- Strategic recommendations
- Actionable plans

---

## Decision Tree: 3 Steps to Choose the Right Tool

### Step 1: What Do You Want to Understand?

**User Level** (individual motivations and behavior) → AI Interview
**Market Level** (trends, competitors, opportunities) → AI Research

---

### Step 2: What Depth Do You Need?

**In-Depth Interviews** (dig into "why") → AI Interview
**Comprehensive Analysis** (market + users + trends) → AI Research

---

### Step 3: Time and Scope?

**Quick Focus** (3-4 hours, 5-10 people) → AI Interview
**Comprehensive Analysis** (2-4 hours, multiple data sources) → AI Research

## Real Scenario Comparisons

### Scenario 1: New Product Launch

**Need**: A coffee brand plans to launch sparkling coffee

#### Using AI Interview

**Questions**:
- Users' first impression of sparkling coffee?
- In what scenarios would they purchase?
- Price acceptance level?

**Output**:
- In-depth interviews with 5-10 users
- Discovered "unwilling to take risks in afternoon energy scenarios"
- Recommend repositioning to morning exploration scenarios

**Advantages**:
- ✅ Quickly validate product concept
- ✅ Deeply understand user psychology
- ✅ Can immediately optimize product

---

#### Using AI Research

**Questions**:
- Sparkling coffee market size and growth trends?
- Main competitors?
- Target audience profile and needs?

**Output**:
- Market analysis report
- Competitive comparison and differentiation strategy
- Target audience and marketing recommendations

**Advantages**:
- ✅ Comprehensive understanding of market environment
- ✅ Strategic-level decision support
- ✅ Includes market + users + competitors

---

### Comparison Summary

| Dimension | AI Interview | AI Research |
|-----------|--------------|-------------|
| **Focus** | User psychology and behavior | Market environment and strategy |
| **Depth** | ★★★★★ (individual) | ★★★★☆ (comprehensive) |
| **Breadth** | ★★★☆☆ (5-10 people) | ★★★★★ (multiple data sources) |
| **Duration** | 50-70 minutes | 2-4 hours |
| **Use For** | Product testing, user insights | Market analysis, strategic planning |

**Recommendations**:
- Before product launch: First use AI Research to understand market, then AI Interview to validate product
- Feature optimization: Directly use AI Interview to deeply explore user needs

## Scenario 2: Entering New Market

**Need**: A fitness app plans to enter the senior fitness market

#### Using AI Interview

**Questions**:
- Senior users' attitudes and needs regarding fitness?
- What are the barriers to using the app?
- Which features would they pay for?

**Output**:
- In-depth interviews with 5-10 senior users
- Discovered "worried about complex operations" and "don't know how to start"
- Recommend adding "beginner guidance" and "simplified interface"

**Use For**:
- ✅ Understand specific needs of new market users
- ✅ Discover product adaptation barriers
- ✅ Quickly validate feature hypotheses

---

#### Using AI Research

**Questions**:
- Senior fitness market size and growth potential?
- Main competitors and market gaps?
- Senior users' health needs and purchasing power?

**Output**:
- Market analysis report
- Competitive landscape and opportunity points
- Entry strategy and risk assessment

**Use For**:
- ✅ Assess market opportunities and risks
- ✅ Develop entry strategy
- ✅ Predict market reactions

---

### Comparison Summary

**AI Interview**: Answers "What do senior users specifically need?"
**AI Research**: Answers "Is the senior fitness market worth entering?"

**Best Practice**:
1. First use AI Research to assess market opportunities
2. After deciding to enter, use AI Interview to deeply understand user needs
3. Design product based on Interview insights

## Combined Usage Strategies

### Strategy 1: Research → Interview

**Use For**: Entering new market, launching new category

**Process**:
1. **AI Research** (2-4 hours):
   - Analyze market size and trends
   - Identify competitors and opportunities
   - Determine target user groups

2. **AI Interview** (50-70 minutes):
   - In-depth interviews with target users
   - Validate Research findings
   - Optimize product design

**Advantages**:
- Macro first, then micro
- Avoid blindly entering market
- More precise product design

---

### Strategy 2: Interview → Research

**Use For**: Product optimization, user insights

**Process**:
1. **AI Interview** (50-70 minutes):
   - Discover user pain points
   - Understand deep needs

2. **AI Research** (2-4 hours):
   - Analyze market trends
   - Validate universality of needs
   - Develop strategic direction

**Advantages**:
- Start from users
- Deepen understanding then expand
- Avoid directional errors

## Common Questions

### Q1: Can both be used simultaneously?

**Yes**. Recommended strategy:
- First use AI Research to understand market
- Then use AI Interview to deeply explore users
- Or vice versa

---

### Q2: Limited budget, can only choose one?

**Depends on your core question**:
- If mainly focused on **user psychology and needs** → AI Interview
- If mainly focused on **market opportunities and strategy** → AI Research

**Examples**:
- Product feature optimization → AI Interview
- New market entry evaluation → AI Research

---

### Q3: Does AI Research include AI Interview?

**Partially included**. AI Research will:
- Reference some AI persona interview content
- But not complete in-depth interviews
- If deep exploration needed, recommend using AI Interview separately

---

### Q4: Which is more suitable for startups?

**Depends on stage**:
- **Product validation stage**: AI Interview (quickly validate hypotheses)
- **Market exploration stage**: AI Research (assess opportunities)

**Budget Considerations**:
- AI Interview lower cost (¥50-100)
- AI Research more comprehensive but slightly higher cost (¥200-300)

---

## Final Takeaway

> "AI Interview digs into user depth, AI Research analyzes market breadth.
> Choose Interview for user insights, Research for strategic decisions."

**Remember**:
- ✅ AI Interview: Explore "why", in-depth interviews
- ✅ AI Research: Analyze "what", comprehensive research
- ✅ Best practice: Research macro + Interview micro
