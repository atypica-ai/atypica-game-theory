## atypica's Unique Value

### 1. Deep Insights, Not Just Trend Data

**GfK's strengths**:
- GfK Consumer Life: World's most comprehensive consumer trend research (since 1997)
- 25 countries, 30K+ consumers annual data
- Retail data measurement (tech/durable goods markets)
- Long-term trend analysis and forecasting

**GfK's limitations**:
- Tells you "consumer trends" and "market data"
- Can't tell you "why this user won't buy your product"
- Macro data, not individual insights

**atypica's capability**:
- AI deep interviews (dig into "why")
- 3000+ word conversations per person
- Understand individual purchase decision processes
- Discover unexpected barriers and opportunities

**Case comparison**:

**Using GfK**:
```
Consumer Life data (annual):
- Trend: Consumers more concerned about sustainability (+15% YoY)
- Tech products: Smart home interest rising (+20%)
- Retail data: Category growing 12%

→ Know "market trends," don't know "why my product isn't selling"
```

**Using atypica**:
```
Deep interviews with target users:
User A: "Sustainability is important, but your product packaging
       is all plastic, contradicting 'sustainable' values."

User B: "I'm interested in smart home, but your APP
       requires 3 account registrations, too troublesome, gave up."

User C: "The trend exists, but your pricing $299,
       competitor $199 with similar features, not sure if extra $100 is worth it."

→ Real problems:
  - Not lacking market demand (GfK data correct)
  - But product packaging, usage barriers, pricing have issues
→ Solutions:
  1. Switch to sustainable packaging
  2. Simplify registration (one-click login)
  3. Pricing $249 or add unique features
→ Expected: 3x sales increase
```

**The difference**: GfK tells you "market trends," atypica tells you "why your product isn't capturing the trend."
