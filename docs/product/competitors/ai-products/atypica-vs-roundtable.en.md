## Real-World Case

**Background**: New product choosing among 3 positioning directions.

**Traditional approach** (needs Roundtable protection):
- Week 1-2: Recruit 60 people (20 people/direction)
- Week 3: Distribute surveys
- Problem: Encountered 10% fraudulent responses
- Use Roundtable Proof of Human to filter
- Week 4: Data analysis
- Output: Positioning A (75% acceptance) > Positioning B (65%) > Positioning C (55%)
- Cost: Recruitment fees $3K-6K + Roundtable API fees + 4 weeks time

---

**Using atypica**:
- Day 1: AI interviews with 60 people (3 hours)
- Validation: Positioning A indeed most popular
- But deep interviews discovered:
  ```
  User A: "Positioning A sounds good, but I'm concerned about price.
         If it's over $25, I might not buy."

  User B: "Core Feature X in Positioning A attracts me,
         But Feature Y you emphasize, I don't care about.
         Marketing focus is wrong."

  Real insights:
  - Pricing ceiling $25 (psychological barrier)
  - Marketing focus should be Feature X not Y
  - Add unexpected Feature Z demand
  ```
- Strategic recommendations:
  ```
  1. Price at $22 (avoid $25 barrier)
  2. Marketing highlight Feature X
  3. Add Feature Z
  → Expected: Conversion rate from 5% to 12%
  ```
- Cost: Subscription fee + 3 hours

**atypica's value**: Not just choosing the best direction, but optimization and execution plans.
