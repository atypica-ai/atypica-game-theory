# atypica vs Dovetail: User Research Platform vs Insight Management Tool

## Key Difference in One Line

**Dovetail helps you "manage and analyze existing interview data," atypica helps you "generate AI users and execute research."**

---

| Dimension | Dovetail | atypica |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| **Positioning** | Insight management and analysis platform | AI user research platform |
| **Core Capability** | Auto-transcription + AI theme extraction + knowledge base | AI interviews + discussion + observation + research |
| **Data Source** | Requires importing real-person interview recordings/text | AI actively generates users and executes research |
| **AI Role** | Analysis tool (analyzes existing data) | Research tool (generates data) |
| **Prerequisites** | Must have real-person interview data first | No prerequisites needed |
| **Time** | Depends on real-person interview duration | 3-4 hours for complete research |

| **Use Cases** | Enterprise-level team collaboration + knowledge management | Rapid user research + concept validation |

## atypica's Unique Value

### 1. Active Research, Not Just Analysis

**Dovetail's premise**: You've already completed real-person interviews, need analysis.

**atypica's capability**:
- No need for real-person interviews
- AI actively generates users
- AI executes deep interviews
- 3-4 hours for complete research

**Process comparison**:

**Using Dovetail**:
1. Recruit 10 real people (1-2 weeks)
2. Execute interviews (2-3 days)
3. Upload to Dovetail (1 hour)
4. AI analyzes and extracts themes (automatic)
5. **Total time**: 2-3 weeks

**Using atypica**:
1. Plan Mode designs research (30 minutes)
2. AI executes 10-person deep interviews (2 hours)
3. AI auto-generates insights (30 minutes)
4. **Total time**: 3-4 hours

**The difference**: Dovetail needs you to have data first, atypica helps you generate data.

## Common Questions

**Q: Dovetail also has AI, why still need atypica?**

**Different AI applications**:
- Dovetail's AI: Analyzes existing data (transcription + theme extraction)
- atypica's AI: Generates data (leads research)

**Analogy**:
- Dovetail: AI helps you organize notes
- atypica: AI does the research for you

---

**Q: When do you really need Dovetail?**

If you:
- ✅ Have dedicated user research team
- ✅ Conduct many real-person interviews monthly
- ✅ Need team collaboration and knowledge management
- ✅ Need to integrate multiple research tools

But if you need rapid validation, no real-person interview data, limited budget → atypica is more suitable.

---

**Q: Can you use both?**

Yes, combine by phase:
1. **atypica rapid exploration**: Validate direction, filter hypotheses
2. Real-person interviews validate key issues
3. **Dovetail knowledge management**: Long-term accumulation and team collaboration

This avoids wasting real-person interview budget on wrong directions.

## Final Takeaway

> **Dovetail manages your research data, atypica helps you generate research data. No matter how good management is, without data it's useless.**

**atypica's core value**: Rapidly validate direction before spending money on real-person interviews.
