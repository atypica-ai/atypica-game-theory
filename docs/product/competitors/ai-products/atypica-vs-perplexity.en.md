**Background**: New SaaS product positioning decision.

**Using Perplexity** (3 minutes):

---

**Using atypica** (3 hours):
```
Deep interviews with 20 target users:

User A: "I know the features Perplexity mentioned,
       but my real pain point is:
       Existing tools are too complex, team won't use them.
       I need a 'ready in 5 minutes' simplified version."

User B: "'Time tracking' sounds good, but my team resists,
       feels like being monitored. What I need is 'progress transparency'
       not 'time surveillance'."

       'asynchronous collaboration' (different time zone teams),
       existing tools are all real-time collaboration designs."

Real insights:
- Market exists (Perplexity right)
- But real demand isn't "more features"
- But "simpler" + "asynchronous collaboration"

Positioning decision:
"For 5-20 person remote teams, ready in 5 minutes"
Highlight: Simple + asynchronous collaboration
→ Result: 1000+ paying users in 3 months post-launch
```

**Combined value**: Perplexity tells you "where the market is," atypica tells you "how to differentiate and win users."
