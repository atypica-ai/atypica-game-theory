## Real-World Case

**Background**: New brand choosing among 3 positioning directions.

**Using PersonaPanels**:
- Test 3 positionings (hours)
- Result: Positioning A (82% preference) > Positioning B (68%) > Positioning C (55%)
- Recommendation: Choose Positioning A
- Launch result: Market acceptance matches prediction, but conversion rate lower than expected

---

**Using atypica**:
- Same validation of 3 positionings (3 hours)
- Validation: Positioning A indeed most popular (PersonaPanels prediction accurate)
- But discovered:
  ```
  Conversion barriers:
  User A: "Positioning A sounds good, but I'm concerned about price.
         If it's over $25, I won't buy."

  User B: "Core selling point X in Positioning A attracts me,
         But Y you emphasize, I don't care about.
         Marketing focus is wrong."

  Optimization direction:
  - Pricing ceiling $25 (psychological barrier)
  - Marketing highlight selling point X not Y
  - Add social attributes to packaging (users want to share photos)
  ```
- Strategic recommendations:
  ```
  1. Price at $22 (avoid $25 barrier)
  2. Marketing focus on selling point X
  3. Package design adds sharing elements
  4. Convenience stores primary channel (breakfast scenario)
  → Expected: Conversion rate doubled
  ```
- Launch result: 90% acceptance, conversion rate increased 2.5x

**Core value**: Not just prediction, but optimization and execution plans.
