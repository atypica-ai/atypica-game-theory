# Plan Mode: Let AI Think Before Acting

**One-line summary**: Plan Mode is the intent clarification layer where AI automatically generates a complete research plan with methods and steps, which you review and confirm before execution begins.

---

## Why Do You Need Plan Mode?

### The Problem with Traditional AI

**Direct execution, black box operation**:
- User: "I want to understand Gen Z's attitudes toward sustainable fashion"
- AI: Starts executing immediately (user doesn't know what it will do)
- Result: Might be going in the wrong direction, wasting time and budget

### Plan Mode's Solution

**Show the plan first, then execute after confirmation**:
- User: "I want to understand Gen Z's attitudes toward sustainable fashion"
- AI: Generates a 5-step research plan
  1. Define target user (Gen Z, 18-25 years old)
  2. Monitor social media (Little Red Book/Bilibili discussions)
  3. Group discussion (8 people discussing sustainable fashion)
  4. In-depth interviews (5 people exploring purchase intent)
  5. Generate research report
- User: Reviews plan → Confirms or modifies
- AI: Begins execution

## Real Case Study: Bubble Coffee Product Validation

### User Input (Initial Request)

```
I want to launch a bubble coffee product targeting young professionals,
but I'm not sure whether to emphasize health or social attributes.
```

**Problem**: Vague requirement, not sure where to start

---

### Plan Mode Generates Plan

AI automatically analyzes the requirement and generates:

**Research Plan: Bubble Coffee Product Positioning Validation**

**Goal**: Determine product positioning (health vs social vs other)

**Steps**:

1. **Define Target User Characteristics**
   - Demographics: 25-35 years old, first-tier city professionals
   - Screening criteria: Regular coffee consumers, willing to try new products
   - Estimated participants: 8 people

2. **Market Trend Analysis**
   - Use Product R&D Agent
   - Analyze coffee market trends
   - Output 3 possible positioning directions

3. **Group Discussion to Test Positioning**
   - Use Discussion
   - 8 people discuss 3 positioning directions
   - Quickly identify the most popular direction

4. **In-Depth Interviews to Verify Motivation**
   - Use Interview
   - 1-on-1 interviews with 5 high-intent users
   - Deep dive into why they like this positioning

5. **Generate Research Report**
   - Product positioning recommendations
   - Target user persona
   - Pricing and packaging suggestions

**Estimated time**: 8-12 hours
**Expected output**: Complete product positioning report

---

### User Reviews Plan

**After seeing the plan**:

✅ **Satisfied aspects**:
- Research steps are clear
- The sequence of discussion before interviews makes sense

⚠️ **Need adjustments**:
- "25-35 is too broad, I want to focus on 28-32-year-old women"
- "Can we add packaging design testing?"

**User modifications**:
1. Adjust target user: 28-32 years old, female professionals
2. Add step: Upload 3 packaging design mockups, test feedback via Discussion

---

### AI Updates Plan

Based on user feedback, AI automatically updates:

**Research Plan: Bubble Coffee Product Positioning Validation (v2)**

**Goal**: Determine product positioning + packaging design validation

**Steps**:

1. Define Target User Characteristics
   - Demographics: **28-32 years old, first-tier city female professionals** (modified)
   - Screening criteria: Regular coffee consumers, value product aesthetics
   - Estimated participants: 8 people

2. Market Trend Analysis (same as above)

3. Group Discussion to Test Positioning (same as above)

4. In-Depth Interviews to Verify Motivation (same as above)

5. **Packaging Design Testing** (new)
   - Upload 3 packaging design mockups
   - Discussion about which is more appealing
   - Output packaging recommendations

6. Generate Research Report (updated: includes packaging recommendations)

**Estimated time**: 10-14 hours
**Expected output**: Product positioning report + packaging design recommendations

**User**: "The plan looks good, let's start executing"

**AI**: Immediately begins execution according to the confirmed plan

> 4. Discussion to test new positioning
> 5. Generate brand perception report

## Plan Mode vs Direct Execution

| Dimension | Plan Mode | Direct Execution |
|-----------|-----------|------------------|
| **Transparency** | Fully transparent, see all steps | Black box, don't know what AI is doing |
| **Controllability** | Can modify plan, flexible adjustments | Can't adjust, only restart |
| **Risk** | Catch problems early, zero-cost corrections | Problems discovered after execution, resources wasted |
| **Collaboration** | Team can review plan | Hard to explain to others |
| **Use cases** | Complex projects, first-time use, team collaboration | Simple tasks, experienced users |
| **Time** | +5-10 minutes review time | Immediate execution |

**Recommendation**: Use Plan Mode in most cases to avoid wrong direction

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: How Much Extra Time Does Plan Mode Add?

**Plan review time**: 5-10 minutes
**Value**: Avoid wrong direction, save hours to days

**Calculation**:
- Without Plan Mode: Execute for 8 hours, discover wrong direction, restart = 16+ hours total
- With Plan Mode: Review 10 minutes + execute 8 hours = 8.17 hours total

**Conclusion**: Plan Mode actually saves time

- Modify research steps
- Adjust target users
- Add/remove research methods
- Change number of participants and scope

**Cannot customize**:
- Can't ask AI to do research methods it doesn't support

### Q3: Can I Regenerate the Plan If I'm Not Satisfied?

**Yes**:
- Click "Regenerate"

- Can regenerate multiple times

**Recommendation**:
- For small adjustments, modify directly (faster)
- For completely wrong direction, regenerate

**Not mandatory**:

**But strongly recommended**:
- Use Plan Mode on first use to understand the process
- Use Plan Mode for complex projects to avoid errors
- Use Plan Mode for team collaboration to reach consensus

### Q5: How Long After Plan Generation Do You Need to Confirm?

**No time limit**:
- Plan is saved in system
- Can review anytime
- Can share with team for discussion before confirming

**Recommendation**:
- Confirm same day to avoid losing context
- Important projects can discuss for 1-2 days

## Practical Tips

### 1. Must Use Plan Mode on First Use

**Reasons**:
- Understand how atypica.AI works
- Learn how to submit better requirements
- Avoid wasting resources due to unfamiliarity

**Experience**:
- 1st time: "Oh, that's how it works"
- 3rd time: Start understanding how to submit requirements
- 10th time: Can consider skipping Plan Mode

---

### 2. Must Use Plan Mode for Complex Projects

**What's a complex project**:
- Involves 3+ research steps
- Needs multiple research methods
- Limited budget or time requiring precise control

**Value**:
- Optimize research path in advance
- Avoid discovering wrong direction mid-project
- Save time and cost

---

### 3. Must Use Plan Mode for Team Collaboration

**Reasons**:
- Need to explain research plan to boss/colleagues
- Need team discussion and consensus
- Avoid post-execution disputes

**Workflow**:
1. Researcher uses Plan Mode to generate plan
2. Export plan document
3. Team meeting review
4. Confirm execution after approval

---

### 4. Can Skip Plan Mode for Simple Tasks

**What's a simple task**:
- Only needs 1-2 steps
- Research direction is very clear
- Experienced user

**Examples**:
- "8 people discuss this product concept"
- "Monitor Little Red Book for camping-related discussions"

**Can execute directly, no need for Plan Mode**
