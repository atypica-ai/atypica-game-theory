# 1. 猎鹿 Tournament Rules — Official Version
N Players: Min 4, Up to 10 per group  ·  Rounds: 3
Each round, secretly choose: STAG or RABBIT

Rabbit: You always receive 10 points, regardless of what anyone else does.
Stag (succeed): If >=T players in your group also chose STAG, every stag hunter receives 25 points.
Stag (fail): If <T players chose STAG, every stag hunter receives 0 points.
T = roundup(40% * N) eg. 4 for 10 players, 4 for 9, 4 for 8, etc.
Discussion rounds: 1
After each round: Everyone sees who chose what and what each player scored.
Winner: Highest cumulative score after 3 rounds advances. Ties broken by most stag attempts (rewards boldness over safety).
