What are Real Person Agents?

Real Person Agents are high-fidelity consumer models built using the methodology from the Stanford Town paper (Generative Agents: Interactive Simulacra of Human Behavior). Through AI-conducted in-depth interviews lasting 1-2 hours with real consumers, we generate an average of 5,000 words of transcript for each person, creating complete "digital twins" with unprecedented individual decision-making simulation accuracy.

These agents are not mere data aggregations, but intelligent entities capable of exhibiting consistent personality traits and decision-making logic in new contexts, providing new possibilities for solving complex problems (wicked problems) in business and social domains.

Construction Methodology

1. In-Depth Interview Design

Based on the American Voices Project

This is a joint project by Stanford and Princeton universities, specifically designed to collect in-depth personal narratives nationwide. Our interview structure covers four core dimensions:

  • Life Journey Narrative: Important turning points, setbacks, and achievements
  • Value Exploration: Deep understanding of family, work, and social responsibility
  • Social Viewpoint Expression: Political leanings, social issue attitudes, future expectations
  • Decision Pattern Analysis: Thought processes and weighing factors in specific contexts

Application Scenarios

Ideal Application Scenarios

  • • Early Exploration: Product concept validation, market reaction testing
  • • Cross-Cultural Insights: Global market entry strategies, localization plans
  • • Rapid Iteration: A/B test pre-screening, creative concept evaluation
  • • Hard-to-Reach Groups: High-net-worth individuals, specific professional group research

Application Limitations

  • • Complex Behavior Observation: Requires real product usage experience
  • • High-Risk Decisions: Critical business like major product launches
  • • Deep Emotional Insights: Qualitative research like brand emotional connections
  • • Real-Time Interaction: User experience research requiring immediate feedback

Technology Outlook

Consumer Understanding in the Age of Agents

Atypica.AI represents a new phase in consumer insight analysis—transitioning from passive analysis relying on historical data to proactive simulation based on AI agents. It's not meant to completely replace traditional market research methods, but to serve as a powerful complementary tool, especially when rapid, cost-effective preliminary insights are needed.

In this business world filled with complex problems, Real Person Agents provide a new approach to problem-solving: through deep simulation of AI agents, we can better understand the "why" behind consumer behavior, enabling more informed business decisions.
